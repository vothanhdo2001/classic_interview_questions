## BODY - 6 Classic Questions

### Q1: "Tell me about yourself"
- Keywords: **30 seconds · introduction · 3 things**
- Formula: Background → Main skill → Why this job

### Q2: "Why do you want this job?"
- Keywords: **research · connect · stand out**
- Do: Find 1 real thing about the company → connect to your own story

### Q3: "What is your greatest strength?"
- Keywords: **one strength · real story · result**
- Formula: Name your strength → Back it up with a story
- Example: staying calm → deadline moved up → finished on time

### Q4: "What is your greatest weakness?"
- Keywords: **honest · real weakness · working to fix**
- Don't: lie ("I work too hard")
- Do: choose a real weakness + show what you are doing about it
- Example: avoided hard conversations → started asking for feedback → got better

### Q5: "Tell me about a mistake you made"
- Keywords: **not a trap · chance to show who you are**
- Formula: Mistake → What I learned → What I changed → Better result

### Q6: "Do you have any questions for us?"
- Keywords: **don't say "no" · end strong · look to the future**
- Power question: "Is there a problem your team is facing right now that I could help with?"
